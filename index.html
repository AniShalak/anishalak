Premised on the notion of ‘regenerative utility’, leather goods label Yu Mei calibrates the codes of luxury and functionality, always with reverence for the environment. Founder Jessie Wong partnered with us to realise a comprehensive, ongoing rebrand grounded in the Yu Mei brand pillars of materiality, practicality and regeneration. With a view to accentuating premium functionality within a coherent aesthetic ecosystem, we took cues from the vocabulary of archiving and indexing to build a design system balanced on elegance and practicality.
